how to play

the first thing you will need to see is the word you are looking for, and most of the time it will be a word that is related to programming(css, html, javascript, python, ect).
the second thing you need to know is that the word will be scrambled up(html becoming thml, javascript becoming tavajsipr, ect).

the last thing you will need to know is that when you click on the correct scrambled word(i.e: you clicked tavajsipr when you needed to find a scrambled version of javascript),the word will become highlighted.

another thing i need to tell you is that there are different levels of word search difficulty(i.e: level 1 can be the word css scrambled to scs, but level 5 can be javascript scrambled into tavajsipr).

ok, now start playing!
