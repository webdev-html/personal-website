iframes


what is an iframe


an iframe is basiclly the tag for basic embedding in html, and what it does it that adding the most important property of the iframe tag of "src", and setting that property to a valid webpage, in the webpage the iframe tag is on, it will make a small version of the webpage that your embedding.


when to use an iframe


you should use iframes for embedding videos, audio files, or just small parts of other website, like if your making a website all about harry potter, you could add an iframe of the author(j.k rowling) talking about harry potter.

propertys of iframes


the most important property of an iframe is the "src" property, and the reason it is so important is because it tells the iframe what to embed, such as google.com or something else. another important property of an iframe is the "border" property, and the reason it is so good for the iframe, is becuase since the iframe is just a box with something from another website in it, the border should look good, so that it stands out, it is not as important as telling the iframe the thing that it should embed, it is still good in modern web development to add a nice and clean border.
